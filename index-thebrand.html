攘夷志士 - Four Heavenly Kings - The Last Generation

Because of the arrest of Yoshida Shouyou, his students rose up and sparked the last revolt, lead by three of his students: Sakata Gintoki, Katsura Kotarou and Takasugi Shinsuke. These three as well as the later addition, Sakamoto Tatsuma, would become known as the Last Generation as well as being dubbed the Four Heavenly Kings. Gintoki earned the nickname Shiroyasha (White Demon), feared by comrades and enemies alike for his demon-like fighting style. Takasugi created and led his own force called the Kiheitai (Demon Army) and became known for his successful battle strategies. Katsura earned the mocking nickname, Runaway Kotarou, for his tactical retreats that minimize ally losses and create better battle plans; he also earned the nickname The Noble Youth of Madness for his poise in battles. And Sakamoto, with the nickname The Dragon of Katsurahama, was able to raise money, weapons and men for the rebel cause.

Sakata Gintoki

坂田 銀時

Gintoki Sakata is the main protagonist of the Gintama series. He is the founder and president of the Yorozuya, as well as a highly-skilled samurai, having fought in the Joui War in the past. During the war, he became known as the Shiroyasha (literally meaning "White Yaksha", a kind of demon in Buddhism and Hinduism) due to his powerful swordsmanship, demonic appearance, white clothing, and silver hair.

"Before dawn, the night is darkest just before dawn. But keep your eyes open, keep them open no matter how dark the night ahead may be. If you avert your eyes from the dark, you'll be blind to the rays of a new day"

Takasugi Shinsuke

高杉 晋助

Takasugi Shinsuke was the leader of the Kiheitai (Army of Demon Soldiers), an extremist Joui faction and a wanted fugitive. He was the main recurring antagonist in Gintama before the appearance of Utsuro, later becoming Gintoki's ally again. Takasugi learned of the strange school Shouyou opened that teaches poor children, Shouka Sonjuku and decided to join, helping to save Shouka Sonjuku from being shut down.

"This left eye has closed forever, bearing your tearful face as the last image it saw. Don't let your grief stricken face be the last thing printed into my right eye before it's closed forever as well"

Katsura Kotarou

桂 小太郎

Katsura Kotarou is the leader of a moderate Joui faction and a fugitive wanted by the authorities. His grandmother taught him that in order to be a great general, one must be more careful and more fearful in order to survive and lead the war. Then he became attracted to Shouyou's philosophy. When Shouyou was arrested, Katsura joined the ongoing Joui War with Takasugi, Gintoki to save him.

"I can't die before I see the dawn of Edo. My eyes may have failed me but I can still properly see, I can still see the path we must take"

Sakamoto Tatsuma

坂本 辰馬

Sakamoto Tatsuma is the leader of the trading fleet Kaientai. Despite being a pacifist and a rich kid, he decided to join in the Joui War, acting as a supplier for the samurai. Seeing his comrades die one by one, Tatsuma decided to leave the Earth, fulfill his dream of travelling through the galaxy and find a way that would benefit both humans and the Amanto. During the war, his right arm was gravely injured in an attempt to carry an injured enemy to safety, giving him permanent damage and making him unable to use a sword.

"Sorry guys, right now I don't really care whether we win or lose this war. Forget about wining and losing, right now I'm getting to have a wild time with those no-good brats"
